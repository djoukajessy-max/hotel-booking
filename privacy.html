Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to MyHotel. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website, booking system, or contact our support team.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with us:

  • Full name

  • Phone number
  • Payment details (processed securely)
  • Booking preferences (room type, dates, special requests)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • To process your bookings and payments

  • To improve our services and website
  • To send you booking confirmations and important updates

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may only share information in the following cases:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors, IT support) who help us operate our business

  • To protect the safety and security of our guests and property

5. Data Security

We use secure servers and encryption methods to protect your information. However, please note that no online transmission can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.
